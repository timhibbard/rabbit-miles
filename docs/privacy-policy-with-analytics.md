## Privacy Policy for RabbitMiles (with Plausible Analytics)

**Last updated:** [Date when changes go live]

### Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At RabbitMiles, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. 
This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

---

### What Data We Collect

When you connect your Strava account to RabbitMiles, we collect:

- Your Strava profile information (name, profile picture)
- Your activity data (GPS tracks, distance, time, date)
- OAuth access tokens to securely access your Strava data

---

### How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

- Analyze your activities against the Swamp Rabbit Trail GPS coordinates
- Calculate your personal totals and projections
- Display your statistics on your personal dashboard
- Show your totals on leaderboards (only if you opt in)

---

### Data Sharing

**We do not sell your data.** Your activity details remain private by default.

If you opt in to leaderboards, only your chosen display name and trail totals are visible to other users. 
Your detailed activity information, GPS tracks, and other personal data remain private.

---

### Data Storage and Security

We store your data securely using industry-standard encryption. OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest, 
and we never store your Strava password.

---

### Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data:

- **Disconnect:** You can disconnect your Strava account at any time from the Settings page
- **Delete:** When you disconnect, your data is deleted from our system within 30 days
- **Opt-out:** You can leave leaderboards at any time without disconnecting
- **Access:** Contact us to request a copy of your stored data

---

### Third-Party Services

RabbitMiles uses Strava's API to access your activity data. When you connect your account, 
you agree to Strava's terms and privacy policy as well. We recommend reviewing 
[Strava's Privacy Policy](https://www.strava.com/legal/privacy).

---

### Analytics and Usage Data

**[NEW SECTION - Only applicable if implementing Plausible Analytics]**

To understand how people use RabbitMiles and improve the service, we use Plausible Analytics, 
a privacy-friendly analytics service.

**What Plausible Collects:**
- Page views and visitor counts (anonymous, aggregated)
- Traffic sources (which websites refer visitors)
- Browser type and operating system (for compatibility)
- Country-level geographic data (for localization)
- Feature usage (like "Strava Connect" button clicks)

**What Plausible Does NOT Collect:**
- Personal information or user IDs
- Cookies (Plausible is completely cookie-free)
- IP addresses (anonymized immediately)
- Cross-site or cross-device tracking
- Any data that could identify you

All analytics data is aggregated and anonymous. No information can be used to identify you personally.

Learn more about Plausible's privacy practices: [https://plausible.io/data-policy](https://plausible.io/data-policy)

---

### Error Monitoring

**[NEW SECTION - Only applicable if implementing Sentry]**

To improve service reliability and fix bugs quickly, we use Sentry for error monitoring.

**What Sentry Collects When an Error Occurs:**
- Error message and stack trace
- Browser type and version
- Page where error occurred
- Time of error

**What Sentry Does NOT Collect:**
- Personal information (we configure Sentry to exclude this)
- User credentials or tokens
- Activity data or GPS information
- Browsing history

Error data is used solely to diagnose and fix technical issues. We retain error data for 90 days.

Learn more about Sentry's privacy practices: [https://sentry.io/privacy/](https://sentry.io/privacy/)

---

### Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for essential functionality (maintaining your session).

We use privacy-friendly analytics that do not require cookies or collect personal information. 
We do not use third-party advertising cookies or invasive tracking technologies.

**Cookies we use:**
- **Session cookie (rm_session):** Essential for keeping you logged in (HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax)
- **OAuth state cookie (rm_state):** Temporary cookie for Strava authentication security (deleted after use)

You can disable cookies in your browser, but this will prevent you from using RabbitMiles.

---

### Children's Privacy

RabbitMiles is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect 
data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

---

### International Data Transfers

RabbitMiles is hosted on AWS servers in the United States. By using our service, you consent to your 
data being transferred to and processed in the United States.

---

### Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** We retain your data while your Strava account is connected
- **Disconnected accounts:** Data deleted within 30 days of disconnection
- **Backups:** May be retained for up to 90 days for disaster recovery
- **Analytics:** Anonymous, aggregated data retained indefinitely
- **Error logs:** Retained for 90 days

---

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes 
by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

If we make material changes that reduce your privacy protections, we will notify you via email 
(if you've provided one) or through a prominent notice in the application.

---

### Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

**[Only required if you have EU users]**

For users in the European Union, we process your data based on the following legal grounds:

- **Consent:** You provide explicit consent by connecting your Strava account
- **Legitimate Interest:** Analytics and error monitoring to improve service quality
- **Contract:** Necessary to provide the service you've requested

You have the right to withdraw consent at any time by disconnecting your account.

---

### Your EU Privacy Rights

**[Only required if you have EU users]**

If you're in the European Union, you have these rights under GDPR:

- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format
- **Right to Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

---

### California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

**[Only required if you have California users]**

California residents have the right to:

- Know what personal information we collect
- Know whether we sell personal information (we don't)
- Opt-out of sale of personal information (not applicable)
- Request deletion of personal information
- Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights

**We do not sell personal information.**

For privacy-specific inquiries or to exercise your data rights, you can also email: 
[privacy@rabbitmiles.com] (if you set up this email)

### Enforcement and Complaints

If you believe we've violated your privacy rights, you may:

1. Contact us directly using the information above
2. File a complaint with your local data protection authority (EU)
3. File a complaint with the California Attorney General (California residents)

## Comparison: Current vs. With Analytics

| Section | Current Policy | With Analytics | Impact |
|---------|---------------|----------------|---------|
| **Third-party services** | Strava only | Strava + Plausible + Sentry | Low |
| **Cookies** | Session only | Session only (Plausible is cookie-free) | None |
| **Data collection** | Activity data only | Activity data + anonymous usage stats | Low |
| **Privacy level** | Very high | High | Minimal reduction |
| **Transparency** | Excellent | Excellent | Maintained |
| **User trust** | Maximum | High | Slightly reduced |

---

## Key Takeaways

✅ **Privacy-friendly analytics (like Plausible) have minimal impact**
- No cookies
- No personal data
- No tracking
- Fully transparent

✅ **Error monitoring (like Sentry) is beneficial**
- Improves reliability
- Can be configured privacy-first
- Users benefit from fewer bugs

❌ **Traditional analytics (Google Analytics) would be problematic**
- Requires cookies
- Collects personal data
- Requires consent banners
- Conflicts with current privacy promise

---

**Recommendation:** If implementing analytics, use Plausible + Sentry with the privacy 
policy updates shown above. This maintains RabbitMiles' privacy-first commitment while 
gaining valuable insights.
